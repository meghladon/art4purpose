About

Art is already valuable. We just let it flow.

ART4PURPOSE is a platform where artists share and sell original work — and 100% of the sale price goes directly to charity. No platform fees. No commissions. No middlemen.

We don't believe art needs to be justified by its utility. The creative process is intrinsically valuable — to the person making it, to the person encountering it, to the culture it lives in. What we do believe is that when art moves between people, it can carry more than beauty. It can carry care, connection, and material support to the places that need it.

ART4PURPOSE is an ecosystem, not a transaction. Artists share work they believe in. Collectors find something that moves them. And the value that exchange generates flows onward — into Australian organisations doing vital work in literacy, conservation, housing, and mental health.

Radical transparency

Every exchange is traceable. You can see where the value flowed, which community received it, and when.

Artist-led

Artists set their own prices and choose their own causes. This is their practice, their generosity, their terms.

100%, always

Not 80%. Not 90%. The full sale price goes to charity. ART4PURPOSE operates through separate funding, not artist revenue.

Abundance over scarcity

This isn't about extracting value from art. It's about recognising the abundance that already exists in creative communities and letting it circulate.

ART4PURPOSE acknowledges the Traditional Owners of the lands on which we work and create. We acknowledge that sovereignty was never ceded, and pay our respects to Elders past, present, and emerging.

Our partner charities

Where the value flows

Every charity on ART4PURPOSE is an Australian organisation doing vital, verified work. When an artist shares their work and a collector takes it home, the value of that exchange keeps moving — into these communities.

📖
Indigenous Literacy Foundation
Getting books and literacy resources to children in remote Indigenous communities across Australia. Since 2011, ILF has distributed over 350,000 books to communities in need.
🌿
Australian Conservation Foundation
Australia's national environment organisation — protecting land, water, wildlife and climate through advocacy, campaigns, and community action since 1966.
🏠
Homes for Homes
An innovative model that channels voluntary property sale donations into social and affordable housing. Addressing Australia's housing crisis through systemic change.
🤝
Beyond Blue
Supporting all Australians affected by anxiety, depression and suicide. Providing information, support services, and funding vital research into mental health.
